Overall Structure

The IIT Madras BS Degree Program offers a unique structure with four progressive levels. Students can exit at various points to earn a Foundational Certificate, Diploma(s), BSc Degree, or the full four-year BS Degree in Data Science and Applications. This modular approach ensures flexibility, allowing learners to advance at their own pace and choose the exit option that best fits their goals.

  • Foundation Level: 32 Credits (8 Courses)
  • Diploma Level: 54 Credits (12 Courses + 4 Projects)
  • BSc Degree Level: 28 Credits (Total 114 Credits so far)
  • BS Degree Level: 28 Credits (Total 142 Credits)

Completion time ranges from 4 to 8 years based on personal pace and performance in assessments. Learners may exit earlier by completing relevant credits and earn appropriate certification from IIT Madras.

Term Structure

Each year is divided into three terms of four months each: January Term, May Term, and September Term. Each term includes 12 weeks of coursework, followed by in-person invigilated quizzes and End Term Exams. Assessments may include programming exams, mini projects, vivas, and take-home assignments.

Course Registrations

Learners can register for up to four courses per term based on their Credit Clearing Capability (CCC). The CCC is determined by performance in the Qualifier Exam or the previous term’s End Term Exams. The Foundation Level CCC is 4, and the Diploma Level CCC is also 4.

Foundation Level courses must be completed before enrolling in Diploma Level courses, and Diploma Level courses must be completed before proceeding to the Degree Level. This ensures a seamless transition and adequate preparation at every stage.

Assessments

There are three primary types of assessments for each course:

  • Weekly Assignments (online)
  • Monthly in-person Quizzes
  • In-person End Term Exams

Additional components may include mini projects, programming exams, vivas, or take-home assignments, depending on the course.

Exam Cities

In-person Quizzes and End Term exams are conducted in multiple cities across India. For learners based outside India, there are exam centers in Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, and UAE. Remote proctored exams are allowed for learners who are unable to attend in person; traveling learners must pin their location when taking internet-based exams.

If a student is abroad but plans to be in India for an exam day, they should notify the institute in advance to arrange a seat at one of the exam centers. Violation of guidelines may be considered malpractice and result in withheld exam results.

Foundation Level

The Foundation Level focuses on Mathematics, Statistics, Basics of Programming, and English. After completing all 8 Foundation Level courses successfully, students can either exit with a Foundational Certificate from IITM CODE or proceed to the Diploma Level.

  • Mathematics for Data Science I & II
  • Statistics for Data Science I & II
  • Computational Thinking
  • Programming in Python
  • English I & II

Students are evaluated through weekly assignments, quizzes, and end-term exams. A strong grasp of fundamentals is essential for moving ahead to higher levels.

Diploma Level

The Diploma Level comprises two tracks:

  • Diploma in Programming
  • Diploma in Data Science

Each Diploma is 27 credits (6 courses + 2 projects + 1 skill enhancement course). A learner can complete one or both Diplomas before moving on to the BSc or BS levels. Alternatively, they can exit with a Diploma from IIT Madras in either Programming or Data Science or both.

Courses for Diploma in Programming (27 credits):

  • Database Management Systems
  • Programming, Data Structures & Algorithms using Python
  • Modern Application Development I & Project
  • Programming Concepts using Java
  • Modern Application Development II & Project
  • System Commands

Courses for Diploma in Data Science (27 credits):

  • Machine Learning Foundations
  • Business Data Management & Project
  • Machine Learning Techniques
  • Machine Learning Practice & Project
  • Business Analytics
  • Tools in Data Science

BSc Degree Level

Once a learner completes all courses in the Foundation Level (32 credits) and the Diploma Level (54 credits), they may opt to pursue the BSc Degree (28 credits). This level includes advanced topics in Data Science and Programming, preparing the learner for industry or higher studies.

Upon completion of 114 total credits, you can either continue to the BS Degree level or exit with a BSc Degree in Programming and Data Science from IIT Madras.

BS Degree Level

The BS Degree level (28 additional credits, total of 142) builds on the BSc core, focusing on specialized and advanced subjects in Data Science and its applications. Successful completion of these credits awards the BS Degree in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras.

This comprehensive four-year degree is designed to provide ample knowledge and hands-on experience in domains of Programming, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and more, equipping students with career-ready skills.

Degree Level Courses

At the BSc and BS Levels, a variety of core and elective courses are offered, including:

  • Core Courses:
  • Software Engineering
  • Software Testing
  • AI: Search Methods for Problem Solving
  • Deep Learning
  • Strategies for Professional Growth
  • Elective Highlights:
  • Big Data and Biological Networks
  • Advanced Algorithms
  • Linear Statistical Models
  • Large Language Models
  • Privacy & Security in Online Social Media
  • ...and many more

Students can tailor their studies by choosing electives that match their interests and career goals. Many courses offer project-based learning for hands-on skill development.
