Term Structure

Every year is divided into three terms of four months each: January Term, May Term, and September Term. Each term includes 12 weeks of coursework, two in-person Quizzes, and one End Term Exam.

Course Registrations

In each term, learners may register for up to 4 courses. Two foundational courses must be taken in the first term, and one or two additional courses can be added, up to the maximum of 4 per term.

Some courses have a project component worth 2 credits. Registering for such courses automatically includes the project. These can be taken concurrently or in a later term.

Assessments

There are 3 types of assessments for each course:

  • Online Weekly Assignments
  • Monthly in-person Quizzes
  • In-person End Term Exams

Additional components may include programming exams, mini projects, vivas, or other take-home assignments.

Exam Cities

The in-person Quizzes and End Term Exams are conducted in multiple cities across India. For learners based outside India, there are exam centers in Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, and UAE. Other international learners may take remote proctored exams.

On exam day, students who write internet-based exams must declare their location. If you plan to be in India for an exam, please notify us in advance so we can arrange a seat at an Indian exam center. Violation of these norms may be considered malpractice.

Fee Structure

You pay only for the courses you register for each term. Below is the fee breakdown:

  • Foundational courses: ₹4,000 per course
  • Skill Enhancement courses: ₹7,500 per course
  • All other diploma courses: ₹10,000 per course
  • Projects: ₹2,500 per project

Additional exam fees apply for exams taken outside India. Fee waivers are available based on socio-economic background.
