Eligibility

Any learner who has:

  • completed any UG degree through any mode
  • or completed at least two years of any UG degree through any mode before January 2025

Mandatory Requirements to Enroll and Learn

  • Access to good internet connection and a laptop/desktop device is crucial to learn effectively. Refer to this document for complete software and hardware requirements.
  • Learner must be able to travel to assigned exam centres for quizzes and exams each term. Check the exam cities section in the Academics page for the current list of exam centres.

Application Process

Application for the first batch starting January 2025 is currently closed. Interested in the next batch of admissions? Fill this form and we will notify you.

Anyone who is eligible may apply by filling in the application form, uploading required documents, and paying the application fee.

Category of Applicant Application Fee
General Category / OBC Applicant ₹6000
SC / ST Category / PwD (≥ 40% disability) ₹3000
SC / ST Category Applicant who is ALSO PwD (≥ 40% disability) ₹1500

All applicants should attend the in-person Qualifier Exam and obtain the required cutoff marks to join the program.

Within India: Exams are conducted in designated centres in 110+ cities under invigilation.
Outside India: Applicants opting to write the Qualifier Exam outside India must pay an additional exam fee. Where exam centres exist, candidates must write in person; otherwise, they appear for remote proctored online exams.

Qualifying Criteria

To pass the Diploma Qualifier Exam, learners must achieve both:

  • A minimum Total Qualifier Exam Score
  • A minimum Qualifier Exam Score in each subject
Category Min. Required Total Score Min. Required Score in each Subject
General Learner 50% 40%
SC / ST / PwD* 40% 30%
SC / ST + PwD* 40% 30%
OBC-NCL / EWS* 45% 35%

*Note: Relaxations in pass criteria indicated for various categories apply ONLY for the qualifier exam. No relaxations once registered in the program.

Only those who pass the Qualifier Exam will be eligible to register for the respective Diploma Program. Learners who pass on 22 December, 2024 can join in January 2025 term only.

International Students

This program is open to learners from all around the globe. You can study from IIT Madras and earn a Diploma in Programming or Data Science irrespective of the country you are from.

IIT Madras is setting up in-person exam centres in various countries. Currently, we conduct in-person exams in:

  • UAE - Dubai, Sharjah, Abu Dhabi
  • Sri Lanka - Colombo, Jaffna
  • Bahrain - Manama

Students residing in these locations must attend in-person exams, roughly once a month. Others can enroll and appear for remote proctored exams. Please write to ge@onlinedegree.iitm.ac.in if you have further queries.
